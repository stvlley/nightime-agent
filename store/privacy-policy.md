# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Nitime ("Nitime", "we", "us") provides a messaging workspace for independent providers. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices users have.

Replace `<support-email>` before hosting this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

We collect the email address, business name, display name, and authentication information needed to create and operate a provider account.

### Provider Workspace Content

We store information providers enter or import, including FAQ answers, profile fields, connected channel metadata, message threads, provider replies, approval status, and uploaded conversation files selected by the provider.

### Client Message Content

When a provider connects a messaging channel or web chat link, client messages sent through that channel may be stored so the provider can review, approve, and reply from the Nitime workspace.

### Subscription Information

For App Store subscriptions, we store product IDs, transaction IDs, entitlement status, plan type, verification date, and expiration or renewal metadata when available. Apple processes App Store payments. Nitime does not receive full payment card numbers for App Store purchases.

### Direct Payment Links

Providers may store their own PayPal, Venmo, Cash App, or Zelle handles in the app. Payments through those services happen outside Nitime. Nitime does not process, escrow, or settle client service payments.

### Device And Usage Information

We may collect app usage events needed for app functionality, reliability, security, debugging, subscription entitlement, and product improvement. Optional analytics should be enabled only after consent where required.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide the provider inbox and web chat workspace.
- Route messages from connected channels.
- Generate FAQ and reply suggestions from provider-approved content.
- Save drafts and approval decisions.
- Verify subscription entitlements.
- Maintain provider public profile pages when enabled.
- Provide customer support and troubleshoot channel setup.
- Protect the service from abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.

Nitime does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, legal advice, tax advice, or financial advice.

## AI-Assisted Features

Nitime may use automated systems and language models to draft replies or suggest FAQ entries. Providers remain responsible for reviewing replies, setting policies, and deciding what is sent to clients. Sensitive or uncertain replies should be reviewed manually.

## Sharing Information

We share information only as needed to run the service:

- Hosting, database, authentication, and edge-function providers.
- Messaging and channel providers connected by the provider.
- App Store services for subscription verification.
- Support and security vendors when needed to operate the service.
- Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law.

We do not sell provider or client message content.

## Data Retention

We retain account, workspace, message, and subscription records while the account is active or as needed for service, legal, tax, audit, security, and backup purposes. Providers may request deletion by contacting `<support-email>`.

## Account Deletion

To request account deletion, contact `<support-email>` from the account email address. Include "Delete my Nitime account" in the subject. We will delete or de-identify eligible account data, subject to legal, security, billing, and backup retention requirements.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards. No internet service is completely secure. Providers should avoid importing or storing unnecessary sensitive client information.

## Children

Nitime is intended for adult providers and is not directed to children. Do not use Nitime if you are not legally allowed to manage the provider workflow in your jurisdiction.

## International Use

Information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. By using Nitime, you understand that information may be processed outside your region.

## Changes

We may update this policy. If changes are material, we will provide notice through the app, website, or account email.
